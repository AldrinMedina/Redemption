OUR PLATFORM

🛠️

Quality of Life (QoL) Improvements

We believe that small, consistent changes can make a big difference in the daily lives of students. From improved access to facilities and resources to enhanced communication between the council and the student body, we are committed to making every student’s campus experience smoother, more comfortable, and more productive.

🎯

Expansion of College Activities

We aim to create more opportunities for students to grow, connect, and have fun through new and diverse activities. By introducing workshops, competitions, outreach programs, and interest-based events, we will ensure that students can find something that inspires them outside the classroom.

🌟

College Week & College Night

We will bring back the excitement and community spirit that make our college life memorable. Our goal is to make College Week bigger and better — filled with engaging activities, performances, and camaraderie — and to end it with a grand College Night celebration that everyone will look forward to year after year.
